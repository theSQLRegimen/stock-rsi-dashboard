Project:  Stock Market RSI Analytics Dashboard

Problem

I lead a trading group and wanted a way to analyze all stocks in the S&P 500 and NASDAQ using technical and fundamental indicators in one centralized dashboard.

The goal was to identify stocks that:

Have an RSI (Relative Strength Index) near or below 30
Were profitable in the previous fiscal year
Are trading significantly below their 52-week high

I also wanted a fast and interactive way to evaluate any ticker individually and measure the magnitude of its pullback from recent highs.

Solution

Built a unified analytics platform that ingests, transforms, stores, and visualizes market and financial data for U.S. equities.

Features
Coverage of all S&P 500 and NASDAQ-listed stocks
Daily market data ingestion
RSI indicator calculation and interpretation
Revenue and net income reporting from the previous fiscal year
Net margin percentage calculation
52-week high tracking
Pullback magnitude categorization
Interactive ticker-level analysis dashboard
Automatic exclusion of tickers with incomplete Yahoo Finance data

Tech Stack
Data Engineering
Python for data ingestion and transformation
MSSQL for relational data storage and modeling
Analytics & Visualization
Power BI for dashboarding and interactive analysis
